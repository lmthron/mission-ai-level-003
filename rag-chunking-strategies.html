üí° Key Insight: Overlap Strategy

Most chunking implementations include 10-20% overlap between chunks to prevent information loss at boundaries. This ensures concepts spanning chunk boundaries aren't lost. For example, with 300-word chunks and 50-word overlap, the last 50 words of Chunk 1 appear as the first 50 words of Chunk 2.

*NVIDIA research showed semantic chunking can improve retrieval accuracy by up to 9% over fixed-size chunking.
